## Post-Session Questions / 会后问题

1. What was the most valuable part of the workflow? / 这个流程中最有价值的部分是什么？
2. What was hardest or least clear? / 哪个部分最困难或最不清楚？
3. In your own words, what does the opportunity score tell you? / 请用自己的话说明机会评分告诉了你什么。
4. What would you do after exporting the brief? / 导出简报后你会做什么？
5. Would local-only storage change when or how you use this tool? Why? / 仅本地存储是否会改变你使用这个工具的时间或方式？为什么？
