# M7 Participant Recruitment Brief / M7 参与者招募说明

> **Inactive optional template / 未启用的可选模板:** Do not use this invitation or contact participants unless the owner explicitly reactivates real-user validation for this project. / 除非负责人明确重新启用本项目的真实用户验证，否则不得使用本邀请或联系参与者。

## Who We Need / 招募对象

Three independent developers or product-focused builders who have chosen software side projects and did not help build AI Idea Hunter.

招募三名曾经选择软件副项目、且未参与 AI Idea Hunter 开发的独立开发者或产品型开发者。

## English Invitation

> I’m testing AI Idea Hunter, a local-first workspace for evaluating software ideas. I’m looking for a 30–40 minute usability session where you use a real but non-confidential idea, share your screen, and explain what you expect. We are testing the product, not your ability. Participation is voluntary, you can stop at any time, and only anonymized notes will be added to the public project. No recording is planned by default.

## 中文邀请文案

> 我正在测试 AI Idea Hunter——一个用于评估软件创意的本地优先工作区。现招募参与者完成一次 30–40 分钟的可用性测试：你将使用一个真实但不涉密的创意、共享屏幕，并说出自己的预期。我们测试的是产品，而不是你的能力。参与完全自愿，你可以随时停止；公开项目中只会加入匿名笔记。默认不进行录制。

## Screening Questions / 筛选问题

1. Have you chosen or evaluated a software project in the last year? / 过去一年中，你是否选择或评估过软件项目？
2. What do you currently use to capture or compare ideas? / 你目前使用什么方式记录或比较创意？
3. Did you help design, implement, or review AI Idea Hunter? / 你是否参与过 AI Idea Hunter 的设计、开发或评审？
4. Can you use a sanitized idea without employer, client, or personal secrets? / 你能否使用不包含雇主、客户或个人秘密的脱敏创意？
5. Are you comfortable with anonymized task notes being synthesized in a public repository? / 你是否同意将匿名任务笔记汇总到公开仓库？

Eligible participants answer yes to questions 1, 4, and 5, and no to question 3. Experience with similar tools is useful context, not a pass/fail criterion.

合格参与者需要对问题 1、4、5 回答“是”，对问题 3 回答“否”。类似工具的使用经验只作为背景信息，不影响是否合格。
